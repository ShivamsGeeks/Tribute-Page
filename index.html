Nawazuddin Siddiqui

Faizal Khan of Gangs of Wasseypur
Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Ideal for all upcoming actors.
  • 1974 - He born on 19th May 1974.
  • After 12th - He went to National School Of Drama in New Delhi.
  • After Graduation - He moved to Mumbai.
  • 1999 - He made his Bollywood debut in 1999 with a small role in Aamir khan starrer Sarfarosh and He then appeared in Ram Gopal Varma's Shool.
  • 2000 - Then appeared in Jungle.
  • 2003 - Rajkumar Hirani's Munnabhai MBBS.
  • 2018 - He won too many awards but mainly Best Actor at the Asia Pacific Award for Outbreaking Performance In Manto.
